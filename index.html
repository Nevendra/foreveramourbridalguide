Start the Quest for Your Dress . . .

What’s your style, season, and theme?

  • Beach
  • Ball Room
  • Barn
  • Loft

Know silhouettes: Most Important factor in dress selection

  • Silhouettes define the dress and should compliment your body and style.
  • Try on different silhouettes to pick the one that is the best for you.

Find Your Style

  • Create an inspiration board of dresses on Pintrest you to share with your bridal consultant.
  • Understand thats dresses look different on model than they do on you.

Set a budget and commit to it!

  • Budget for alterations, which are an additional charge.
  • Common alteration include Hem, Bustle, and taking the dress in to fit you perfectly.
  • Average price, is $600.

It’s all in the timing

Timing is essential for dress shopping

  • Allow 1 year to 8 months to shop.
  • Add 3 months for alterations.
  • Have less time? Salons have dresses that you can buy off the rack.
  • Check online for sample sale and trunk shows.

  • Book an appointment in advance.
  • Ask the price range of the dresses.
  • Share details and Pintrest/ photos about your wedding and dresses you love!

Who to shop with

  • Shop with 1 or 2 close friends or family members.
  • You can lose your voice in a large entourage of opinions.
  • It's you day and your dress so it's your decision you decide.

Share with those who couldn't come

  • Use Face Time with family who live too far to be with you at the salon.
  • Take photos to capture the joy and excitement of our journey.

Bring the little things

Wear appropriate undergarments

  • Strapless bra is essential.
  • Rock your spanx.
  • All of the above effect the fit of the dress.

Accessories for the final touch.

  • Keep in mind your finishing touches (veil, headband).
  • Bring shoes with the same heel height you may wear.
  • Wear little/no make-up to keep dress samples clean.

Bring camera and have a friend snap pics!

It’s time to try on dresses!

About the dress an You

  • Try on the styles you already have in mind.
  • And keep an open mind!!!
  • Understand that the dresses you try on will not be your exact size.
  • Don’t get discouraged.

Making Appointment

  • Don’t go to more than 3-4 salons.
  • It gets overwhelming.
  • Know the type of store your confortable shopping in large or small, salon, and botique.
  • Find out if the store is to your liking.
